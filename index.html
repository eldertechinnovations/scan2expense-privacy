scan2expense-privacy Privacy Policy for Scan2Expense Last updated: December 2025 Scan2Expense (“the App”) is a lightweight utility application designed to extract expense information from receipt images. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled. This Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user. Information We Collect Scan2Expense is designed to operate without user accounts, subscriptions, or tracking. a. Receipt Images and Extracted Data When you take a photo of a receipt or upload an image: The image is processed only for the purpose of extracting expense information such as vendor name, date, total amount, tax, and currency. Receipt images and extracted data are not permanently stored by the app. Processing occurs securely and is limited to the time required to generate the expense summary. b. Personal Information We do not collect: Names Email addresses Phone numbers Account credentials Payment information The app can be used fully without providing any personal identifying information. How Information Is Used Any data processed by Scan2Expense is used solely to: Extract expense details from a receipt image Display the extracted information to the user Allow the user to copy or export the expense summary (for example, as text or CSV) The data is not used for: Advertising Analytics Profiling Marketing Training AI models on user-specific data Data Storage and Retention Scan2Expense does not store receipts or extracted expense data after processing is complete. We do not maintain user histories or logs tied to individual users. Any temporary data required for processing is discarded immediately after use. Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is not shared with: Advertisers Data brokers Analytics providers Social networks If third-party services are used solely to perform receipt text extraction, they are used only as data processors and are not permitted to retain or reuse data for other purposes. Tracking and Cookies Scan2Expense: Does not use cookies Does not include analytics SDKs Does not track user behavior Does not fingerprint devices Children’s Privacy Scan2Expense is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue. User Control and Responsibility You remain in full control of: Which receipts you scan Whether you choose to copy or export extracted data How you store or use exported expense information Once data is copied or exported from the app, its handling is the user’s responsibility. Data Security We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data during processing. However, no method of electronic transmission or processing is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements.
